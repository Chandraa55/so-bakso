Bakso Malang Definition.

Bakso Malang is a meatball dish that is widely found throughout Malang City and Malang Regency, East Java. Malang meatballs were first introduced by a Chinese-Malaysian immigrant named Tjoen Moesliem in 1939. Tjoen Moesliem opened a meatball stall in the Malang City Square area which later became a famous place to enjoy Malang meatballs. Similar to other meatball dishes, the dish consists of meat formed into balls and noodles. The difference is in the addition of fried wonton skin and fried tofu.

Tools and Materials.

Ingredients for making beef meatballs: a. 500 grams of beef b. 1 egg c. 100 grams of tapioca flour d. 3 cloves of garlic, finely ground e. 1 tsp pepper powder f. 1 tbsp salt g. 1/2 tsp beef stock powder h. Ice cubes to taste i. Hot water Tools for making beef meatballs: a. Chopper b. Spatula c. Bowl d. Spoon e. Plastic gloves f. Pan

How To Make Bakso Malang

How to make beef meatballs: 1. Put all the ingredients into the chopper 2. Grind the ingredients in the chopper until smooth 3. Once smooth, remove the dough from the chopper. Then put the dough into a bowl 4. Prepare hot water in a pan 5. Use gloves to form meatballs 6. Hold the dough and press it until it forms a ball. Then take the formed dough using a spoon 7. Put the formed meatball dough into a pan filled with hot water.
